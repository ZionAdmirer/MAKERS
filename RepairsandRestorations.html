Repairs and Restorations

Repair and restoration services to breathe new life into your favorite garments.

What Are Repairs and Restorations?

At MASTER, we specialize in repairing and restoring garments to their former glory. Our expert team is dedicated to ensuring that your favorite pieces are not only fixed but also enhanced, allowing you to enjoy them for years to come.

Our Approach

We understand the sentimental value of your garments, which is why we take a meticulous approach to repairs and restorations. Our process includes:

  • **Assessment:** Evaluating the damage and discussing your needs and expectations.
  • **Expert Repair Techniques:** Utilizing high-quality materials and techniques to ensure durability.
  • **Restoration:** Bringing back the original beauty of your garments, whether through patching, re-stitching, or other methods.
  • **Final Touches:** Ensuring that the garment is cleaned and pressed before returning it to you.

Types of Repairs and Restorations We Offer

Our repair and restoration services cover a wide range of needs, including:

  • Hemming: Shortening or lengthening hems on pants, skirts, and dresses.
  • Patching: Repairing holes or tears in fabric with matching or contrasting patches.
  • Re-stitching: Fixing seams that have come undone or are fraying.
  • Button Replacement: Replacing lost or damaged buttons with matching ones.
  • Zipper Replacement: Replacing broken zippers to restore functionality.
  • Custom Restorations: Tailoring specific needs to restore vintage or cherished garments.
