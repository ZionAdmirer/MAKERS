Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered!

What services do you offer?
We offer personalized stitching, professional alterations, bespoke tailoring, fabric selection assistance, fashion consultations, and repairs/restorations.
How can I book a service?
You can book a service by contacting us through our website or by calling our customer service number.
What is your cancellation policy?
If you wish to cancel your order, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance to avoid any cancellation fees.
Do you offer custom tailoring?
Yes, we specialize in custom tailoring to fit your unique style and preferences.
How can I contact you for further questions?
You can contact us via our contact page, or reach out through our social media channels.
What types of fabrics do you work with?
We work with a wide range of fabrics including cotton, silk, linen, wool, and synthetic materials. Our team can help you choose the best fabric for your needs.
How long does it take to complete an alteration?
The time required for alterations depends on the complexity of the work. Typically, simple alterations take about 1-2 weeks, while more complex requests may take longer.
Can I bring my own fabric for custom orders?
Yes, you can bring your own fabric for custom orders. Our team will assist you in ensuring it is suitable for the desired service.
